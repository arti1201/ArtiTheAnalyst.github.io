    5. In this project, I designed and developed a dynamic Power BI dashboard to analyze company-wide sales performance across product categories, customer segments, and global regions. This project deepened my Power BI skills, reinforced the importance of clean data and stakeholder-aligned design, and sharpened my ability to communicate insights through effective dashboard storytelling.
