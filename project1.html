Project Overview

This project involved the end-to-end creation of two interactive Tableau dashboards designed for sales managers and executive stakeholders. The primary goal was to translate raw sales and customer data into intuitive, real-time dashboards that drive strategic decision-making.

What I Built

Sales Performance Dashboard

  1. Visualized key sales KPIs: Total Revenue, Monthly Sales Trends, Regional Performance, and Top-Selling Products.
  2. Enabled filtering by region, product category, and sales channels for better exploration.
  3. Added line graphs, bar charts, and heatmaps for better trend and pattern analysis.
    4.

Customer Insights Dashboard:

  1. Highlighted customer demographics, purchase frequency, and churn rates.
  2. Segmented customers by behavior (e.g., loyal vs. one-time buyers).
  3. Identified high-value customers and helped guide retention strategies.
    4.

How I Built It

  1. Data Preparation: Cleaned raw Excel datasets using filters and calculated fields; handled null values and inconsistencies.
  2. Exploratory Analysis:Performed initial EDA in Excel/SQL to identify trends and relevant KPIs.
  3. Dashboard Design:Focused on usability — grouped related metrics, used color-coding for emphasis, and ensured mobile/tablet responsiveness.
  4. Performance Tuning:Optimized dashboard loading time by reducing excessive filters and using efficient data extracts.
    5.

Challenges Faced

  1. Messy Data Inputs: Initial datasets were unstructured with inconsistent date formats, duplicates, and missing customer IDs.
    2. Solution: Applied cleaning functions in Excel and Tableau Prep, and added calculated fields to fill gaps.
  2. Overwhelming Metrics: There was an overload of metrics requested by stakeholders.
    3. Solution: Conducted stakeholder interviews to narrow focus to the most business-impacting KPIs.
  3. Balancing Visual Appeal with Functionality: Ensuring the dashboards looked clean while being technically informative.
    4. Solution: Followed best practices in data visualization (minimalism, logical flow, consistent color palette).

Impact & Outcome

  • Delivered dashboards that helped stakeholders identify underperforming regions and high-potential customers.
  • Improved transparency and data accessibility for decision-makers, leading to more efficient sales reviews.
  • The interactive dashboards replaced multiple static Excel reports, saving time and effort for the analytics team.
