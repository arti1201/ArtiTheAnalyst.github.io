Project Overview

As part of a 3-month internship at Loom Solar Pvt. Ltd., I created two professional Power BI dashboards to support financial transparency and cost optimization across departments. The dashboards provided leadership with real-time access to key financial metrics and business spending.

Dashboard 1: Finance Report

  • Integrated core financial statements:
    1. Profit and Loss Statement
    2. Balance Sheet
    3. Cash Flow Statement
  • Visualized net profit, revenue, operating expenses, and asset-liability distribution.
  • Designed interactive visuals for monthly/quarterly comparisons.
View Dashboard


Dashboard 2: Expense Report

  • Tracked various business expenditures: marketing, salaries, electricity, operations, etc.
  • Enabled filtering by department, time period, and expense type.
  • Helped stakeholders identify cost-heavy areas and potential savings.
