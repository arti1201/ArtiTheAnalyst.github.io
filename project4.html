Project Overview

Designed and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to monitor and analyze company-wide sales performance across different product categories, customer segments, and geographical regions, empowering stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions.

What I Built

Sales Performance Dashboard

  1. Visualized key metrics such as Total Sales (2.3M) and Total Orders (5K) using KPI cards for quick insights.
  2. Created slicers for Year and Category to enable users to dynamically filter and explore data.
  3. Developed a Top Products Table showcasing the highest-selling products with their corresponding revenue.
  4. Built a Sales by Segment donut chart highlighting sales distribution among Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.
  5. Designed a Sales by Category bar chart for easy comparison among Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.
  6. Illustrated Sales Trends Over Years using a line chart to display year-on-year growth.
  7. Integrated a Sales by Region map to visualize regional sales distribution across North America, Europe, and other global regions.
    8.

How I Built It

  1. Data Preparation: Imported the Excel dataset into Power BI, verified data types, corrected any missing values, and ensured consistency across fields like Order Date, Sales, Region, Segment, and Category.
  2. Exploratory Analysis: Conducted an initial analysis to identify the most relevant KPIs and trends, focusing on revenue growth, segment performance, and product profitability.
  3. Dashboard Design: Prioritized user experience by grouping similar metrics together, using consistent color schemes, adding slicers for dynamic filtering, and aligning visuals neatly to ensure a professional and clean look.
  4. Performance Optimization: Created efficient DAX measures (Total Sales, Total Orders) and optimized visual load times by minimizing heavy queries and unnecessary visuals.
